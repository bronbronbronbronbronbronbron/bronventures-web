Entrepreneur, operator, investor.
Building since 12. Shipping since always.

Daniel Bron launched his first venture at 12 and has spent over a decade building across marketing, design, development, fintech, AI, and emerging technology, with a consistent focus on execution that actually works.

By his early 20s, he had already built, scaled, and exited companies, advised major brands and public institutions, and developed a reputation for operating where complexity, technology, and real-world execution meet. His work has included advising DeLorean and Ukraine's Ministry of Digital Transformation.

Today, Daniel leads Fulcrum and Superposition Capital, sitting at the intersection of strategic advisory, company building, and investment. He has spoken at the Javits Center and LA Convention Center, and worked with companies reaching multi-billion-dollar valuations.
