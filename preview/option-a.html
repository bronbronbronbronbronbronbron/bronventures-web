About
The full story.

Daniel Bron is an American entrepreneur, operator, researcher, and investor focused on turning complex technologies into real businesses, systems, and strategic outcomes. He launched his first venture at 12 and has spent the past decade building across marketing, design, development, fintech, AI, and emerging technology, with a consistent focus on execution that actually works.

By his early 20s, Daniel had already built, scaled, and exited companies, advised major brands and public institutions, and developed a reputation for operating where complexity, technology, and real-world execution meet. His work has included advising organizations such as DeLorean and Ukraine's Ministry of Digital Transformation, helping bridge the gap between ambitious ideas and systems that can actually perform in practice.

Today, Daniel leads Fulcrum and Superposition Capital, where his work sits at the intersection of research-led strategic advisory, company building, and investment. He has spoken at venues including the Javits Center in New York and the LA Convention Center, worked with companies that reached multi-billion-dollar valuations, and built his career around one core belief: practical innovation matters more than hype, and execution is what makes ideas real.
