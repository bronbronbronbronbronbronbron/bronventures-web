About ↓
Daniel Bron is an American entrepreneur, operator, researcher, and investor focused on turning complex technologies into real businesses, systems, and strategic outcomes. He launched his first venture at 12 and has spent the past decade building across marketing, design, development, fintech, AI, and emerging technology. Today he leads Fulcrum and Superposition Capital, working at the intersection of research-led strategic advisory, company building, and investment. He has spoken at the Javits Center and LA Convention Center, worked with companies reaching multi-billion-dollar valuations, and is currently a 1L at NSU Shepard Broad College of Law.
