Building since 12.

What I care about is the moment a technology stops being a demo and turns into something a regulated institution can actually run on. That gap is where most of the interesting work in AI is hiding, and it’s where I’ve spent most of the last decade.

I’m a Partner at Superposition Capital, a seed fund investing in AI built for regulated industries, and the founder of Fulcrum, where we build custom AI systems for operations teams that can’t afford for automation to break. I also serve as technical advisor to Solidity Law, a compliance and technology practice for the digital economy, and to Lavr International, a security and logistics firm.

Over the last decade I’ve worked with DeLorean, Ukraine’s Ministry of Digital Transformation, and Lamborghini leadership, was a Thiel Fellowship finalist, and wrote a book about the infrastructure underneath all this called The 4th Industrial Revolution. Based in Miami.

If you’re building something real in that gap, the call button is at the top.
